Seeing college kids struggle with their wadrobe choices gave me the idea to design and develop an iOS app to help them find the right clothing brands for their lifestyle. I led 3 other students to create an app that let users answer several questions and an algorithm determines which brand best fits them. Created using Swift 2, Sketch and artwork designed with Photoshop. Won best-in-session in class of 100 groups.
